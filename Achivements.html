EVENTS Training Project reports News Articles

Achievements in the Financial Year 2020 -21.

  • Vocational training imparted to 693 people, inclusive of 260 women through the network of our 17 clusters.
  • Livelihood projects in NER on cane and bamboo through cluster development projects on traditional bamboo shoot processing, bamboo jewellery, agarbati, bamboo crates, bamboo plantation in NER, incubation centres and hi-tech bamboo nurseries.
  • Awareness programmes on - bamboo treatment and preservation at the plant at the NECBDC campus ground and cleanliness through the sanitization scheme - Swachh Bharat Campaign.
  • Workshops involved capacity building programmes on cane and bamboo products in NER.
  • The Seminar/ training was a certificate programme on ‘Value Addition and Marketing of NTFPS (Plant origin): Bamboo Crafts’ - Assam.
  • Our consultancy covered projects on cane and bamboo products - from construction of facilitation centers, treatment of bamboo at the NECBDC plant to cluster related construction.
  • On the Marketing front, the Virtual Exhibition on “Bamboo Technology” and the North East Festival 2020 Assam saw us carve a niche internationally.

  • NECBDC signed MoUs with -

    • The Government of Jammu and Kashmir
    • The Handicrafts and Handloom department, Govt of Jammu & Kashmir.
    • The ministry of Handlooms and Handicrafts Govt of Jammu & Kashmir - MoU.

Achievements in the Financial Year 2019 -20.

  • NECBDC developed, trained and equipped people of 17 clusters to promote inclusive growth in all NE states.
  • Imparted vocational training to 305 people, inclusive of 53 women.
  • Awareness Programme and capacity building programme on cane and bamboo products in NER
  • The Cane and Bamboo Training cum Technology Park at Jote, Arunachal Pradesh.
  • The Cane and Bamboo Training cum Production Centre at Ukhrul, Manipur.
  • The NEC Guest House, New Delhi received a makeover with eco-friendly resources
  • The Bamboo Auditorium at the National Institute of Technology (NIT) Yupia, Arunachal Pradesh.
  • The Bamboo Treatment Plant & Common Facility Centre (CFC) at Piprakothi, Bihar.
  • Three 'Hi-tech Bamboo Nursery’ projects
  • Constructed bamboo Poly House houses.
  • NECBDC officials went on a study tour to Taiwan.
  • Organized exhibitions, festivals, seminars and workshops to enhance our market visibility.
  • Bamboo Design and Construction workshop in collaboration with the Architect Association of Assam.
  • The Facilitation Centre for Artisans and Weavers at Gorchuk, Guwahati.
  • An MoU was signed for project implementation between National Bamboo Mission Mizoram and CBTC, Burnihat
